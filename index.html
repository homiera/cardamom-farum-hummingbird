How it works

Order

Everything is made to order in small batches. No storefront, no checkout cart. Just DM or email and I'll let you know what's possible, when it'll be ready, and how to pick it up.

Pick up is typically in Alexandria, VA. Location details are shared after order and payment are confirmed. Payment via Venmo or Zelle.

The baker

About Homiera

Baking is how I reset. When everything else is loud, there's something about measuring flour and grinding cardamom that makes the world go quiet again.

Between Bites is my small-batch bakery out of Alexandria, VA. Cookies and pastries made with good ingredients and a lot of intention. Nothing here is mass-produced or made ahead of time. When you order, I make it.

I'm not a storefront. I'm a person who bakes, and I'd love to feed you something.
